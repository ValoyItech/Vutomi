About Nkuna Vutomi

Registered Dietitian Nutritionist

With over 8 years of experience, Nkuna Vutomi is a dedicated registered dietitian specializing in comprehensive nutrition solutions. Her journey began at Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University, where she earned her qualification in Dietetics and Nutrition (2013-2016). Since then, she has been committed to helping individuals achieve optimal health through personalized dietary plans.

Her expertise spans various areas including:

  • Management of chronic conditions like diabetes and heart disease
  • Nutrition therapy for weight management and eating disorders
  • Specialized care for prenatal, pediatric, and sports nutrition
  • Smartwatch sales for health monitoring

Her practice, established in 2018, focuses on providing evidence-based nutrition advice tailored to individual needs. She believes that proper nutrition is the foundation of good health and works closely with clients to develop sustainable lifestyle changes.
